About us

Local care with measurable impact

Men's Health and Food Foundation is a not-for-profit, non-political organization serving health, food security, and human development needs in Edo State.

Mission

Promoting health. Fighting hunger. Building capacity.

The foundation exists to improve lives through disease prevention, general healthcare awareness, emergency food support, counselling, education, skills acquisition, agricultural awareness, and community development.

H

Health and wellbeing

General healthcare education, disease prevention, screening awareness, and referral support.

F

Food and agriculture

Food assistance, agricultural education, food production awareness, and farm product processing advocacy.

S

Skills and protection

Support for orphans, vulnerable people, counselling, education, safety, skills acquisition, and youth career awareness.

Governance and accountability

Built for responsible community service.

The foundation's constitution provides for trustees, regular meetings, prudent application of funds, accurate records, independent audit, and annual returns filing with the Corporate Affairs Commission.

Trustees

Governance is handled by elected trustees in line with the Companies and Allied Matters Act.

Funding

Income may come from donations, grants, charitable assistance, and lawful support from individuals or organizations.

Non-profit clause

Income and property are applied solely toward the foundation's objectives, not distributed as profit to members.
